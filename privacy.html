Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

The Short Version

We don't collect your personal information. We don't track you. We don't sell data. We don't use cookies to follow you around the internet. This site exists to help people find housing resources, and that's all it does.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Browsing behavior, page views, or click patterns
  • Location data
  • Any information you enter into our interactive tools or wizard

Interactive Tools and the Where to Start Wizard

Our Where to Start wizard and other interactive tools on this site (like the Fair Market Rent lookup) run entirely in your browser. Your answers and selections are processed locally on your device to generate personalized recommendations. None of this information is sent to our servers, stored in a database, or shared with anyone. When you close or refresh the page, your answers are gone.

Ask a Question

Our Ask a Question page lets you email us a housing question. When you send us an email, we receive whatever information you choose to include in that message, plus your email address (so we can respond). We use this solely to answer your question. We don't add you to any mailing list, we don't share your email with anyone, and we don't use your question for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect basic server logs (such as IP addresses) as part of its standard hosting infrastructure. We do not access, analyze, or store these logs. You can review Cloudflare's privacy policy for details on their practices.

Our Fair Market Rent and Income Limits tool makes requests to the HUD User API (huduser.gov) to retrieve publicly available housing data. These requests go directly from your browser to HUD's servers. We are not involved in that connection and have no access to it.

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces. Google may collect basic connection information when fonts are loaded. We do not control this. You can review Google's privacy policy for details.

Cookies

This site uses a single cookie to remember your dark mode preference if you toggle it. That's it. We don't use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or tracking cookies of any kind.

Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other analytics or tracking tools. We have no idea how many people visit this site, which pages they look at, or where they come from. That's fine with us.

Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how this site works in a way that affects your privacy, we'll update this page. We don't anticipate that happening — the whole point of this site is to help people, not to collect data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at matt@waythroughproject.com.
