Major Programs We Cover

These are the core affordable housing programs most people are looking for. Each guide walks you through what the program is, who qualifies, and how to apply.

Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers

Rental assistance that caps your rent at 30% of your income. The most common program for individuals and families.

LIHTC (Low-Income Housing Tax Credit)

Below-market-rate apartments that don't require a voucher. You apply directly to the property.

HUD-VASH

Housing and supportive services for veterans experiencing homelessness or at risk of it.

SSI and SSDI

Supplemental Security Income (SSI) and Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) — disability income support that can help you afford housing and basic needs.

Recovery Housing

Peer-supported sober living environments for people in recovery from substance use disorders.

Emergency Rental Assistance

Programs that help cover back rent and utilities when you're behind on payments.
