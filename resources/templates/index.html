Letter & Document Templates

Sometimes you need to put something in writing. Whether you're requesting repairs, appealing a denial, or asserting your rights, having the right words matters. These templates are ready to use—just customize them with your information and send.

Each template includes tips for sending and guidance on what to customize. You're in control. Print them, email them, or send them certified mail. Use these as starting points; adapt them to your situation.

All Templates

Reasonable Accommodation Request

Request housing accommodations for a disability or medical condition. Use this for service animals, accessibility modifications, or other disability-related needs.

Housing Denial Appeal

Appeal a denial for an apartment, subsidized housing program, or housing voucher. Dispute the decision and request reconsideration.

Repair Request Letter

Request that your landlord make necessary repairs to your unit. Creates a written record and demonstrates you gave proper notice.

Security Deposit Demand Letter

Demand the return of your security deposit when a landlord wrongly withholds it. This is your first step before small claims court.

Fair Housing Complaint Narrative

Document housing discrimination based on race, color, religion, national origin, sex, disability, or family status. For filing with HUD or local agencies.

Voucher Portability Request

Request to transfer your housing voucher to a different state or jurisdiction. Use this when moving or seeking to use your voucher elsewhere.
