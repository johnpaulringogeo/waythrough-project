Voucher Portability Request

When to Use This Letter

Use this letter if you have a housing voucher and want to move to a different state, county, or jurisdiction. Voucher portability allows you to transfer your assistance and use it in a new location, rather than losing it when you move.

Common reasons to use portability:

  • You're moving to another state for a job or family reasons
  • You want to move to a lower-cost area
  • You need to relocate for safety or health reasons
  • You want to move closer to family or support services
  • You have a job opportunity in another jurisdiction

Your right to use a voucher elsewhere is called "portability" under the Housing Choice Voucher program. This letter requests to transfer your voucher to a new Public Housing Authority (PHA). Act quickly—there may be waiting lists or timing restrictions depending on how long you've held your voucher.

Pro Tips

  • Check portability policies. Contact your current PHA before moving. Ask about portability rules and whether there are restrictions in the new jurisdiction.
  • Contact the new PHA early. Reach out to the receiving PHA to find out their process and timeline for accepting portability transfers.
  • Don't break your lease early. Make sure your move-out aligns with your lease. You need to inform your landlord at the proper time.
  • Keep documentation. Keep your voucher paperwork, current lease, and all correspondence about the portability request.
  • Follow timelines carefully. Some PHAs have specific windows for portability. Don't miss deadlines or you may lose your voucher.

Template Letter

[YOUR NAME]
[YOUR ADDRESS]
[CITY, STATE ZIP CODE]
[YOUR EMAIL]
[YOUR PHONE]

[DATE]

[CURRENT PHA NAME]
Housing Choice Voucher Program
[CURRENT PHA ADDRESS]
[CITY, STATE ZIP CODE]

Re: Request for Voucher Portability Transfer
Participant: [YOUR NAME]
Voucher Number: [YOUR VOUCHER NUMBER]
Current Address: [YOUR CURRENT ADDRESS]

Dear [PHA DIRECTOR / HOUSING PROGRAM MANAGER]:

I am writing to request a transfer of my Housing Choice Voucher through the portability provision of the voucher program. I am currently a voucher holder with [CURRENT PHA NAME] and wish to move to [NEW CITY/STATE].

Current Voucher Information:

  • Participant Name: [YOUR NAME]
  • Voucher Number: [YOUR VOUCHER NUMBER]
  • Current Address: [YOUR CURRENT ADDRESS]
  • Household Size: [NUMBER OF PEOPLE IN YOUR HOUSEHOLD]
  • Months Held: [NUMBER OF MONTHS YOU'VE HAD THE VOUCHER]

Intended Portability Move:

I intend to move to [CITY], [STATE] and will use my Housing Choice Voucher there. My move date is [PLANNED MOVE DATE]. I am requesting that my voucher be transferred to the [RECEIVING PHA NAME] for continued assistance.

Reason for Move:

[BRIEFLY EXPLAIN YOUR REASON: family relocation, employment opportunity, access to services, safety, etc. EXAMPLE: I have secured employment in [CITY] and will be relocating with my family.]

I understand that portability transfers must comply with Housing Choice Voucher program regulations and the policies of both the receiving and sending PHAs. I request that you process this portability request according to program guidelines and provide me with information about:

  • The timeline for processing my portability request
  • Documentation needed from me
  • Contact information for the receiving PHA in [STATE]
  • Any restrictions or requirements I should be aware of

I am available to provide any additional information needed. Please contact me at [YOUR PHONE] or [YOUR EMAIL] to discuss the next steps in this process.

Thank you for your assistance with this portability transfer.

Sincerely,

[YOUR SIGNATURE]
[YOUR PRINTED NAME]

What to Customize

  • [YOUR NAME], [YOUR ADDRESS], etc.: Your contact information.
  • [DATE]: Today's date.
  • [CURRENT PHA NAME]: The name of your local Public Housing Authority (on your voucher documents).
  • [CURRENT PHA ADDRESS]: The address of your PHA office.
  • [YOUR VOUCHER NUMBER]: Your housing voucher number (on your voucher documents).
  • [YOUR CURRENT ADDRESS]: Where you currently live.
  • [PHA DIRECTOR / HOUSING PROGRAM MANAGER]: Use the name of the director or program manager if you have it; otherwise, "Housing Choice Voucher Program Manager" is fine.
  • [NEW CITY/STATE]: Where you're moving.
  • [NUMBER OF PEOPLE IN YOUR HOUSEHOLD]: Count everyone in your household.
  • [NUMBER OF MONTHS YOU'VE HAD THE VOUCHER]: Calculate from when you received it.
  • [CITY], [STATE]: Your intended destination.
  • [PLANNED MOVE DATE]: When you plan to move.
  • [RECEIVING PHA NAME]: The name of the PHA in the jurisdiction you're moving to.
  • [BRIEFLY EXPLAIN YOUR REASON]: Short explanation of why you're moving.

Before You Send

  • Call your PHA first. Ask about their portability process and requirements. Get the name and address of the person handling portability requests.
  • Find the receiving PHA. Look up the Public Housing Authority in the jurisdiction you're moving to. You'll need their name and address.
  • Check deadlines. Ask if there are specific windows for portability requests or timing restrictions.
  • Gather documents. Have your voucher number, current lease, and move-out date ready.

Tips for Sending

  • Send certified mail with return receipt. This proves the PHA received your request.
  • Keep a copy. Keep a copy of the letter and return receipt.
  • Follow up. Call within a week to confirm they received your request and ask about the timeline.
  • Get everything in writing. Once your portability transfer is approved, get written confirmation from both your current PHA and the receiving PHA.
  • Coordinate with your landlord. Give proper notice to your current landlord (usually 30 days) before moving.
  • Contact the receiving PHA. Once you're approved for portability, work with the receiving PHA to find housing with your voucher in the new location.
