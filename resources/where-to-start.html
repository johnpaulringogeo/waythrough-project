Where to Start?

Not sure which resources fit your situation? Answer a few quick questions and we'll point you toward the most relevant guides and programs.

Let's Find Your Resources

Navigating the affordable housing system can feel overwhelming — there are dozens of programs, each with their own rules, eligibility requirements, and application processes. You shouldn't have to figure it all out on your own.

We'll ask a few quick questions about your situation — usually just 3 or 4 — and then point you toward the specific guides, programs, and next steps that are most relevant to you. Whether you're looking for help for the first time, dealing with a denial, or trying to understand your rights, we'll help you find a starting point.

You can skip any optional question, and you can always start over if your situation changes.

Your answers stay in your browser and are never stored, collected, or sent anywhere. See our Privacy Policy.

Quick Reference

Major Programs We Cover

  • Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers — rental assistance capping rent at 30% of your income
  • LIHTC (Low-Income Housing Tax Credit) — below-market-rate apartments
  • HUD-VASH — housing and services for veterans
  • SSI and SSDI — disability income support
  • Recovery housing — peer-supported sober living
  • Emergency rental assistance — help with back rent and utilities
