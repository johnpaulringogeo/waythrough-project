Housing Authority Directory

Section 8 and public housing are run locally by Public Housing Authorities (PHAs). Each authority has its own waiting lists, its own application process, and its own contact information. These guides summarize an authority's current waiting-list status, programs, and how to apply, and link to the official site for anything time-sensitive.

Waythrough is an independent resource, not a housing authority. We're building this directory out over time — start with our step-by-step guide on how to apply for Section 8, browse resources by city or state, or read how to find your local PHA.
