Letter Generator

Create professional letters to request repairs, accommodations, demand your security deposit back, or appeal decisions. Use these templates to exercise your rights as a tenant or program applicant.

Advocating for your rights doesn't require a lawyer—but it does require clear communication. This tool helps you generate professional letters for common tenant and housing situations. Each letter is a starting point you can customize with your specific details.

Disclaimer: These letters are a starting point. For complex situations, consult a legal aid attorney. Your local legal aid office can provide free or low-cost legal help.
