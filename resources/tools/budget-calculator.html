Monthly Budget Calculator

Figure out where your money goes each month. Track your income, housing costs, and expenses to see if you're in the red or the black—and get practical suggestions for balancing your budget.

A budget isn't about being perfect—it's about knowing the truth of your situation. This calculator helps you see exactly where your income goes and identify areas where you might adjust spending or find additional support.

Start with the numbers you know, fill in your best estimates for the rest, and see what the picture looks like. Everything is private; nothing gets saved or shared.
