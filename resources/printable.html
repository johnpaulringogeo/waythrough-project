Perfect for Case Managers & Social Workers

These one-page guides are designed specifically for professionals who work with clients on housing stability. Print them out, mark them up, share them directly with clients, or use them in training presentations.

Each guide is optimized for printing — no ads, no navigation, no distractions. Just clear, actionable information that fits on a single page or two.

How to Print These Guides

  1. Click any guide from the list above to open it.
  2. Press Ctrl+P (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+P (Mac) to open the print dialog.
  3. Adjust settings if needed (page size, margins, etc.), then click "Print".
  4. The guide will print cleanly — no navigation, no footer, no distractions.
