About This Project

Waythrough Project was started by Matt, who spent years working with the HUD-VASH program helping veterans access housing. After watching too many people struggle with a system that should be helping them — not because they weren't trying hard enough, but because the information was scattered, buried, and confusing — he built this site to put everything in one place.

Everything here is free. There's nothing to buy. We're not selling courses, coaching, or premium access. This is a resource hub, plain and simple. If it helps you take your next step toward stable housing, it's doing its job.
