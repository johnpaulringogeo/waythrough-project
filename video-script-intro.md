# Waythrough Project — Intro Video Script

**Format:** You on camera, talking directly to viewer
**Length:** ~90 seconds
**Tone:** Warm, steady, no shame — like talking to a friend

---

## SCRIPT

If you're struggling to pay your rent or your mortgage right now — I want you to hear something.

You are not alone. And you are not a bad person.

Nearly half of all Americans say they struggle to afford their housing payment. That's not a fringe thing. That's almost one out of every two people.

Right now, over 22 million households in this country are spending more than 30 percent of their income just on rent. And 12 million of those are spending more than half.

More than half their paycheck — just to keep a roof over their heads.

And here's what doesn't make the news: over 770,000 people are experiencing homelessness on any given night. But that number doesn't count the people sleeping on a friend's couch, or staying in their car, or bouncing between family members because they have nowhere else to go. The real number is much higher.

This isn't because people are lazy. This isn't because people made bad choices. Housing costs have outpaced wages for decades, and the safety net has holes in it.

So if you're dealing with this — whether you're on a waiting list, fighting to keep your voucher, or just trying to figure out where to even start — that's why I built this.

Waythrough Project is a free resource. No ads, no paywalls, no one trying to sell you anything. Just real information about affordable housing programs, your rights as a tenant, and how to navigate a system that wasn't designed to be easy.

You deserve to know what's out there. And you deserve someone in your corner.

I'm Matt. Welcome to Waythrough.
