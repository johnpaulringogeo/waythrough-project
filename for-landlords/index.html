Getting Started

To participate in the Housing Choice Voucher program, landlords typically need to:

  • Have a property that meets Housing Quality Standards
  • Sign a Housing Assistance Payments (HAP) lease agreement with the Public Housing Authority
  • Comply with fair housing laws and program requirements
  • Maintain the property according to HQS standards throughout the lease

The application process and specific requirements vary by local Public Housing Authority (PHA). Contact your local PHA to learn about their participation process and current demand for voucher units.
